What I'm Working On

The Unfamiliar City is a catalogue of the beautiful natural spots of the Bay Area. Our YouTube account shows videos of the trail and includes our tips and inside knowledge, our Facebook page connects the community together and posts and shares events having to do with our natural surroundings, and our blog documents the science behind the animals, geography, and flora that we encounter on our journies.
